Viewer/editor supporting ODF and Microsoft files, with E2EE collaboration

Features:
- Supports ODF: odt, ods, odp
- Supports Microsoft files: docx, xlsx, pptx
- Printing
- Online collaboration with E2EE (without accounts)

Uses a "dumb relay server": it only forwards encrypted messages between devices, without storing the actual state of the file, even in encrypted form.
